Welcome to Parasol Navigation!

Parasol helps you to stay comfortable when travelling outdoors by simulating sun and shade along your route and choosing a path that balances distance and your shade preference.

ADDRESS Enter origin and destination addresses here. A marker will appear when you enter the first, and a route when you enter the second
MARKER Marks your origin and destination – drag to adjust as needed.
ROUTE Marks the optimal route – click to get some additional details.
ZOOM Zooms the view in/out
SUN Toggles the simulated sun/shade layer for the current time
TIME Sets the current time, which impacts the optimal route
BETA Sets your sun/shade preference &endash; slide left if your prefer shade, and right if you prefer sun

For the interested, Parasol works by [1] building a high-resolution 3D model of the urban landscape from airborne LiDAR survey data, [2] simulating insolation on that surface based on solar position for the current day and time, [3] interpolating the insolation results to the OpenStreetMaps transportation network, [4] computing a custom cost function given the user's selected shade preference, and [5] computing an optimal path given that cost function.
